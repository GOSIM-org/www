Our Story

The importance of GOSIM

GOSIM (Global Open Source Innovation Meetup) is a global developer meetup created to promote grassroot open-source projects, to facilitate collaboration for these projects, and to help these projects grow into mature stages

    GOSIM supported project characteristics:
  • Global, Innovative, Open-source, Grassroot
  • GOSIM is a free and non-commercial developer event for the open-source developer community, held annually, organized by the open-source community, and for the open-source community. Volunteer-based.
  • Anyone who has the passion for open-source innovation can attend and participate.
  • GOSIM subscribes to an open, diverse, and inclusive culture.
